Eco-Activities

Leave no trace. Discover the wealth of sustainable, low-impact activities you can undertake across Malaysia's varied ecosystems.

The Active
Traveler

Engage with nature responsibly. Our recommended activities support local guides and conservation efforts.

Jungle Trekking & Hiking

From the challenging slopes of Mount Kinabalu to the dense undergrowth of Taman Negara. Hire indigenous guides for the most authentic and safe experience.

BEST FOR: FITNESS & FLORA

Scuba Diving & Snorkeling

Explore the Coral Triangle at Sipadan or Tioman. Ensure you choose dive shops with strict "no-touch" reef policies and eco-certifications.

BEST FOR: MARINE BIOLOGY

Wildlife River Safaris

Take silent electric longboats down the Kinabatangan River to spot Orangutans and Pygmy Elephants without disturbing their habitat.

BEST FOR: PHOTOGRAPHY

Canopy Walks

Experience the rainforest from the perspective of birds and monkeys. Canopy walks are suspended high above the ground to prevent trampling of undergrowth.

BEST FOR: BIRD WATCHING

Caving & Karst Exploration

Wander through the massive chambers of Gunung Mulu National Park. Marvel at millions of bats exiting the caves at dusk.

BEST FOR: GEOLOGY ENTHUSIASTS

Eco-Volunteering

Join conservation programs in Tioman Island or Perhentian to help build artificial reefs, clean beaches, and collect data on marine life.

BEST FOR: CONSERVATION
